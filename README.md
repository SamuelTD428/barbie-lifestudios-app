===================================================================
             SUPER MARIO MASH-UP PACK (BEDROCK PORT)
===================================================================

Thank you for downloading! This directory contains 3 essential files:
1. World Map (.mcworld)
2. Resource/Texture Pack (.mcpack)
3. Custom 40-Skin Pack (.mcpack)

-------------------------------------------------------------------
HOW TO INSTALL (PC & MOBILE)
-------------------------------------------------------------------
1. Download all three files to your device.
2. Double-click or tap each file one by one. Minecraft Bedrock 
   will automatically launch and import them.
3. To play, go to your Worlds list, click the Edit (Pencil) icon 
   next to the Mario world, go to "Resource Packs," and make sure 
   the Mario texture pack is activated for that world!

-------------------------------------------------------------------
CONSOLE LIMITATIONS (XBOX, PLAYSTATION, NINTENDO SWITCH)
-------------------------------------------------------------------
Due to console storage restrictions, you CANNOT download these files 
directly onto a console from a web browser. 

To play this on a console, you must use one of these workarounds:

METHOD 1: LAN / Cross-Play Friend Hosting
- Have a friend on PC or Mobile import and host the world.
- Join their game via LAN or your Minecraft Friends list. 
- Your console will automatically download the textures temporarily.

METHOD 2: Minecraft Realms
- Import the world and packs on a PC or Mobile device.
- Upload the world to a Minecraft Realm on that same Microsoft account.
- Open Minecraft on your console, go to your Realms, and you can play 
  or download the world locally from there!
